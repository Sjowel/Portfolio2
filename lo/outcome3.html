Products Branding

Logos

I started making logos for the project. I drew 7 logos myself and made 1 in Adobe Illustrator. I didn't really do research before I made the logos but I just made what came to my mind. After I made them I got some feedback from Woody, our stakeholder, and he said the V&G looked too professional so he wanted the full name 'Veneman & de Groot'. He liked the Guitar because that's how you see that it has something to do with music. When I got this feedback I went further with creating new logos. These were much better. My design and another logo were chosen as the best logos by a survey. Based on these I made other logos just like my whole group. And out of them, the logo we were going to use for the project was chosen.

Products Portfolio

Typography

To get a good font for my portfolio I tried a lot of different fonts in Figma to see what I liked and what not. I added the 12 fonts I liked most to a sheet and typed text on other sheets to see if I actually liked it if there was written more on a page.

I think this is a good way to find your font for your portfolio because you can really see what you like.
