Iterative design

"You explore and use professional design tools and you iteratively design visual works."

Reflection

How did it go and do you notice any difficulties and challenges?


This actually went great. I realised it is a good thing to make a lot of iterations and ask feedback on it to keep improving your design. the only annoying part is when you ask different people for feedback and they all say something different. Then you have to choose what you like the most and what you think is the best for the project. But it's also a good thing to have different opinions.

Do you want to grow in this learning outcome?


Yes, I want to grow in this learning outcome, I want to ask more feedback to improve my design more then I already do. But I think I already grew compared to last years.
