What is the project about?

For my own project, project X, I am gonna make an app where you can store memories in a time capsule. I am gonna make the app with html, css and javascript. I am gonna make a design in Figma and test it with users. I am made a logo and made different variants of it. I am using version control with Github.

Planning

What did I do?

I made a planning for my project before I started it.

Why did I do that?

I want to know what I have to do and when I have to do it so I can keep track of my progress and if I am done in time.

How did I do it?

I look up how long we had for the project and what I had to do. Based on the time I had I made a planning.

What were the results?

The planning I have now. I didn't make it too complex, just for me to understand and see if I am on schedule and it worked pretty good.

Conclusion

Making a planning is a good idea so you can keep track of your progress and see if you are on schedule.

Research existing apps

What did I do?

I did research to the already existing apps that are similar to the app I want to make.

Why did I do that?

I want to look for inspiration and see what I can do to make my app better than the existing apps.

How did I do it?

I looked into the app store and on internet if I could find something similar of what I wanted to make. But I couldn't so I looked at other apps. I knew Snapchat has a similar feature where you can look back to memories, and BeReal also has kind of the same. So I looked at those apps but they were not quite similar to what I wanted.

What were the results?

Not really what I was looking for, so I had to come up with designs and ideas myself.

Conclusion

You can't always find for what your looking for but that's okay, let your creative mind speak.

App design Figma

What did I do?

Usertests

What did I do?

I did 2 usertests on my app design in FIgma.

Why did I do that?

I want to know if my app is user friendly and if people would like to use it. and to get feedback on my design so I can make it better.

How did I do it?

I made a clickable prototype in Figma and let people do some tasks. I asked them to think out loud so I could see what they were thinking. I also asked them some questions about the design.

What were the results?

They were actually pretty positive about the design I made. The only feedback I really got was to make a button for the "name it" feature, so it was more clear you can click on that to go further. I also got the feedback to make an option to give the time capsules a color but I think that's a thing for the future, and I want to make the app functional first.

Conclusion

I'm happy with the results of the usertests. They were really positive about the design I already have and I am gonna use the feedback I got to improve my design.

App Development

What did I do?

I made the app with html, css and javascript.

Why did I do that?

I didn't know how to make an actual app, so I made a phone on a website and made my app in there.

How did I do it?

I also added a function that if you long press a grid that it gets bigger so you can see your photos or videos bigger instead of in just the small grid. For the rest my design looks kind of the same as my design in Figma.

Conclusion
