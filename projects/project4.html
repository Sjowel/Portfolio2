  • Research

    • Research existing apps

    What did I do?

    I did research to the already existing apps that are similar to the app I want to make.

    Why did I do that?

    I want to look for inspiration and see what I can do to make my app better than the existing apps.

    How did I do it?

    I looked into the app store and on internet if I could find something similar of what I wanted to make. But I couldn't so I looked at other apps. I knew Snapchat has a similar feature where you can look back to memories, and BeReal also has kind of the same. So I looked at those apps but they were not quite similar to what I wanted.

    What were the results?

    Not really what I was looking for, so I had to come up with designs and ideas myself.

    Conclusion

    You can't always find for what your looking for but that's okay, let your creative mind speak.

    Logo design

    What did I do?

    I made a logo.

    Why did I do that?

    I want my app to have a logo so people can recognize it.

    How did I do it?

    I looked up on the internet time capsules for inspiration, based on what I saw I wanted to make a logo. I made it in Adobe Illustrator.

    What were the results?


    This is the current logo I have now. I got feedback from a friend of mine:
    "I like it but add some color to make it more colorful."
    I like that the logo is grey so I can do a lot with the background color of the app I want to make and even make a feature to change the background color because gray always matches with a color.

    Conclusion

    looking online for inspiration for for example a logo is a good idea. don't copy it though, make it original.

    logo variants

    What did I do?

    I made different variants of my logo to use in my app.

    Why did I do that?

    So I can show in the app that the time capsule is opened, closed or if it is time to be opened.

    How did I do it?

    Again I went to Adobe Illustrator and I added a capsule that is closed, and one that is half open so it is ready to be opened.

    What were the results?


    This on the top. I think it is a good idea to have different variants of the logo so you can see if the time capsule is opened or closed. I got the following feedback on this:
    "I like it that you have multiple variants of the logo to show multiple actions."

    Conclusion

    It was a good idea to make different variants of the logo to use by different actions.
