Personal

Helping a classmate

A classmate of mine didn't know how to put her website online on Hera, so I helped her with that. I explained to her how she could do it and I showed her how to do it. I think it's important to help each other because you can learn from each other and you can help each other out when you don't know something.

Asking feedback

For the first 2 months I only thought it was a good idea to ask feedback from only the assessors because they are gonna judge your portfolio. but at my second review I got the feedback to ask more teachers for feedback on my portfolio. So after that I did. I asked different teachers and I got different opinions about stuff from my portfolio. which is sometimes annoying because 1 person likes something else as the other person. Then you have to look what you like yourself and substantiate your choice.
