    • What is the project about?

    For the branding project, we had to make a branding for Woody and Truus (Veneman & de Groot) because they are gonna work together as a duo, so they needed a new branding.

    Research Smartlappen

    What did I do?

    I did some research to the genre Smartlappen. I listened to the music and searched on internet what the genre was. After some feedback I did some research to Neo-Smartlappen and what the difference was between this and Smartlappen.

    Why did I do that?

    I did this research to know what Smartlappen was and what the difference was between Smartlappen and Neo-Smartlappen. This was important because I needed to know what the genre was about so I could make a good branding for Veneman & de Groot.

    How did I do it?

    I listened to the music and searched on internet what the genre was. After some feedback I did some research to Neo-Smartlappen and what the difference was between this and Smartlappen. I put this all into a Word-document.

    What were the results?

    The results were that Smartlappen is a genre of Dutch music.. It is music that is very emotional and is mostly about love and heartbreak. Neo-Smartlappen is a newer version of Smartlappen, it is a little bit more modern and has a little bit more of a pop sound.

    Conclusion

    It is good to do your research if you don't know what something is before you are gonna make designs for it. This way you know what the genre is about and what the target audience is.

    • Research

    Moodboards

    What did I do?

    I made moodboards based on the research I did. I made a moodboard in a little bit of an older style, because that's what I thought Smartlappen was. But when I got feedback from Woody, this wasn't really the direction he wanted to go to. He wanted it to be Neo-Smartlappen, so a lot newer, with bright colors.

    Why did I do that?

    I did this to know what the style of the branding should be. I needed to know what the style was so I could make a good branding for Veneman & de Groot.

    How did I do it?

    I looked for pictures of Smartlappen on the internet and put them in a moodboard. I did this in Figma. After I made my first moodboard I got the following feedback:
    "This is a little bit too old-fashioned, I want you to focus more on the 'neo' part of smartlappen, so the newer part."
    After I got this feedback I did my research to neo-smartlappen and made a new moodboard, this one was into the good direction.

    What were the results?

    A new moodboard with bright colors and a lot newer style. This was the right direction for the branding of Veneman & de Groot.

    Conclusion

    It isn't wrong to make a moodboard in the style you think is correct, but it is important to get feedback on this so you can go further into the right direction.

    Presentation

    Half way through we did a presentation to show what we already got. This is the feedback we got on the sprint presentation:
    - The multicolor rawness in the moodboards is cool and he is curious of this in 'real' content.
    - He thinks 3 to 4 times posting on social media is a lot.
    - He likes to get younger audience to the pub crawl and really wants the Stratumseindgroup to go there.
    - Are all people on social media interested in Smartlappen?
    - We can do a research of the interests of people on Stratumseind.
    - Give your work the attention it needs, don't put every moodboard on 1 page but put them on different pages.

    With this feedback we went further to the final presentation, we explained everything a little bit better, and did better research to the target audience. This is the following feedback we got on our final presentation:
    - Explain the logo better, why did you choose for these colors.
    - Everytime you make a choice you want to do an analyse of why this is the best choice.
    - Give away hoodies so people can see them and want them

    Retrospective

    At the end of this project and at the beginning of the second project we made a retrospective so we could see what we still can improve in the group or what was good and what not. this gave us a nice overview about that.

    • Retrospective

    What did I learn

    I learned to do research about every choice you make, to explain everything clear so the stakeholder and teachers know why you make these choices and just make things. Ask feedback on these things to go further with them. Nothing is wrong. You can just improve everything you make to make it better.
